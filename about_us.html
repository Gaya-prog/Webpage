Who We Are?

Nanofertech is an agritech startup at the forefront of innovation, driven by a bold mission to revolutionize fertilization practices through precision and sustainability. We specialize in cutting-edge controlled-release technology for nitrogen fertilizers and beyond, offering a transformative approach that minimizes waste, maximizes crop yields and places environmental and soil health at the heart of agriculture. Harnessing the power of agro-waste, advanced nanotechnology and AI-powered algorithms, we refine materials to meet the urgent demand for sustainable farming solutions.

What We Do?

Nanofertech specializes in Controlled Release Technology (CRT) for fertilizers, which significantly improves nutrient delivery efficiency to crops. By applying advanced coating materials to fertilizers, the technology ensures controlled release of nutrients directly to the plant over an extended period, in support of growth and development up to maturity and harvest stage. This targeted delivery boosts fertilizer efficiency by up to 95%, reducing nutrient wastage and minimizing environmental impact. The coatings are customizable according to customer preferences, providing tailored solutions that optimize crop yields while promoting sustainable and efficient fertilization practices.

LAMATu, the story behind the name

The story of LAMATu begins with a bond of friendship spanning decades., solidified by passion for sustainable agriculture During a field visit to Tanjung Karang, the co-founders, who have known each other for nearing a decade now, had encountered local farmers to validate their business idea. As they introduced the controlled-release technology and explained how it could eliminate the need for multiple split applications and reduce the overall cost of production, the farmers were taken aback by the innovation. Many of them, in their late 50s, were amazed and responded, “That’s pretty long for a fertilizer,” recognizing the lasting impact it could have on their fertilization regime. This spontaneous remark, inspired the name "LAMATu" derived from the Malay word "lama" meaning "long” with "tu" representing a typical Malaysian expression. The name LAMATu, a perfect reflection of the fertilizer's long-lasting effects was born out of this genuine moment of connection, appreciation and excitement.
