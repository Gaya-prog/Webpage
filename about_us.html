Vision

We envision a future where agriculture transcends mere productivity to become a force of renewal, restoring soil as a vibrant living ecosystem. We champion soil health, recognizing it as the heartbeat of sustainable agriculture and the foundation of prosperity for generations to come.

Mission

Our mission is to transform the status quo of the current fertilization regime by pioneering precision fertilizations that redefine efficiency and sustainability. Rooted in the ethos of “Less for Abundance,” we are committed to lifetime environmental stewardship, protecting and preserving the soil and nurturing the planet.

Who We Are?

Nanofertech is an agritech startup at the forefront of innovation, driven by a bold mission to revolutionize fertilization practices through precision and sustainability. We specialize in cutting-edge controlled-release technology for nitrogen fertilizers and beyond , offering a transformative approach that minimizes waste, maximizes crop yields and places environmental and soil health at the heart of agriculture.

What We Do?

We offer control release technology with targeted delivery that boosts fertilizer efficiency, reducing nutrient wastage and minimizing environmental impact. Our technology is customizable according to customer preferences, providing tailored solutions that optimize crop yields.

Why Us?

Our team brings a rich background in scientific research, deep technical expertise, and a relentless commitment to advancing technology in ways that can make a meaningful difference in people's lives. We firmly believe that the greatest breakthroughs come from marrying scientific excellence with entrepreneurial vision.

LAMATu: The new generation controlled release technology

The story behind the name

The story of LAMATu begins with a bond of friendship spanning decades, solidified by passion for sustainable agriculture. During a field visit to Tanjung Karang, the co-founders, had met local farmers to validate their business idea. As they introduced the controlled-release technology and explained how it could eliminate the need for multiple split applications and reduce the overall cost of production, the farmers were taken aback by the innovation. Among them, they were amazed and responded, “That’s pretty long for a fertilizer,” recognizing the lasting impact it could have on their fertilization regime. This spontaneous remark, inspired the name "LAMATu" derived from the Malay word "lama" meaning "long” with "tu" represents a typical Malaysian expression. The name LAMATu, a perfect reflection of the fertilizer's long-lasting effects was born out of this genuine moment of connection, appreciation and excitement.

The LAMATu Promise

One-time fertilization

Simplifying the fertilization process by ensuring nutrient release is optimized for the crop cycle, cutting down the cost of production.

One-time labour

Reducing the frequency of applications, thereby lowering labour costs and operational complexities for farmers.

Life-time environmental stewardship

Ensuring sustainable farming by leaving no residual pollutants in the soil and protecting the engaging ecosystem.
